Cellular Conn Quality Logger

This is just an app sample for Phonegap Classes. Using HTML+CSS+JS (and Jquery(!)), plus phonegap API's, we built an simple app capable to log, every minute, your cell phone connection plus your Geo position.

We're using the following Phonegap API's:

  • Notification
  • Storage
  • Connection
  • Geolocation
